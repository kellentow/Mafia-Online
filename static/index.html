Welcome to Mafia (WIP)

Mafia is a game about deceit, deduction, and teamwork.

In Mafia, there are several roles:

  • Mafia: The killers. Lie to and deceive your friends to eliminate them all.
  • Innocents: Most of the players. Try to find and vote out the Mafia.
    • Doctor: Stop one person from dying that night.
    • Wizard: Reveal one player's role per night.
    • Muter: Choose one person to mute for the day.
    • Soulmates: Only two players. If one dies, the other dies too.
